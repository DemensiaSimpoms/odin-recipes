Telur dadar

Description

An omelette or fried egg variant is a dish prepared by first whisking the eggs and then frying them with cooking oil or heated butter in a pan.

Ingrediens

  • 2 eggs
  • 50 grams of spinach
  • A pinch of salt
  • A pinch of pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cooking oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the eggs, crack them one by one into a bowl, and then whisk.
  2. Prepare the spinach, wash it thoroughly, and then put it into the bowl. Add salt and pepper, then mix well.
  3. Pour into a non-stick pan and cook until done. Remove from the pan and place onto a serving plate.

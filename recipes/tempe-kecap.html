Tempe kecap (pedas asam manis)

Description

Tempe kecap is a practical home-cooked dish that combines savory tempeh pieces with caramelized sweet soy sauce and spices. It is generally cooked by sautéing sliced shallots, garlic, and chilies, then adding sweet soy sauce and a splash of water so the seasonings absorb perfectly into the tempeh.

Ingrediens

  • 2 blocks of tempeh
  • 7 long red chilies
  • 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar(2 tbsp)
  • 50 ml of sweet soy sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp of seasoning powder
  • 1 tbsp of cornstarch
  • 50 ml of water
  • 20 ml of water to dissolve the cornstarch

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients and cut the tempeh to your liking. Also, slice the red chilies to make them look aesthetic.
  2. Deep-fry the tempeh until golden brown, then drain. Remove the oil from the pan. Be careful, it's hot! I always end up getting burned at this part.
  3. Next, put in the tempeh, sweet soy sauce, sugar, seasoning, and water. Stir well. Then, slowly pour in the cornstarch solution. Cover the pan.
  4. And it's done! The sweet and spicy flavor is so good!
